Clean Sweep Products has been manufacturing quality sweeping compounds and powder soaps for over sixty years and serving industrial distributors all across North America. We are considered an industry leader for our superior quality, fast and reliable service, and our low prices.

We manufacture premium sweeping compounds for any type of floor and are specially formulated with only the best raw materials for maximum dust and dirt control for safe and easy sweeping. Also, our sweeping compounds are brightly colored red or green for spot free sweeping.

Our laundry detergents, oxy stain lift powder, and hand soaps are manufactured with borax and top quailty stain fighting compoinents to effectively and genty clean dirt and stains. All of our soaps are 100% soluble in water, 100% biodegradable, do not contain dyes, is non-toxic, and are safe on drains and plumbing.

Oil-Dri Snow & Go is a unique winter traction aid that breaks down snow and ice while absorbing the melted water before it can re-freeze, it provides instant and long lasting traction. The all-natural formula is non-toxic and contains only 1/3 salt by volume. Don't get stuck without it!

We offer a wide range of packaging choices, and can also provide private labeling.
