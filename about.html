About Us

COMPANY STATEMTNT

WELCOME TO CLEAN SWEEP PRODUCTS

Since 1947, Clean Sweep Products is an American Disabled Veteran/Family owned and operated company. We are the only national sweeping compound manufacturer in the industry today.

We service all National industrial accounts and major retail accounts under our special private labeling program. In addition, we offer special training to our customers on workplace safety and instill knowledge of product.

Over the past years we have stayed ahead of the industry curve by strategically locating our manufacturing facilities in the Northeast (Paterson, NJ), Southeast (Calhoun, GA) , Midwest (Marion, OH) and Southwest (Corsicana, Tx).

Our goal is to create value to our customers by manufacturing a great product, at a great price and having multiple locations provides great freight cost savings to all.

Goal is to provide excellent quality products and service, at a fair price. We offer over 65 years of experience to get the job done right!
