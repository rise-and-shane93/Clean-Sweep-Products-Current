Oil base with grit (regular)

Regular sweeping compound contains sand, which acts as an abrasive to loosen grime and dirt stuck on work floors and it is also effective for collecting and keeping the dust and dirt down while sweeping. This product used by the construction industry, warehouses, boat yards, factories, garages and anywhere heavy-duty cleaning is a must.

Ideal for use on:

  • Unfinished wood
  • Concrete floors
  • Metal surfaces

Oil base: no-grit

No-grit sweeping compound does not contain abrasives, so it will not cause scratching on floors. No-grit is perfect for all types of wood, tile, terrazzo, and finished concrete floors. It collects dirt and dust, and also can be used to absorb liquid spills. Clean Sweep no-grit sweeping compound can be used in stores, warehouses, institutions, churches, restaurants, factories, schools and homes.

Ideal for use on:

  • Ordinary wood and concrete
  • Painted or sealed wood
  • Finished concrete
  • Tile, Terrazzo, and Rubber

Wax Base

Wax base sweeping compound contains wax and does not contain sand, this product conditions floors while it cleans. Wax base is perfect for waxed floors, painted and varnished floors, tile, terrazzo, finished wood and concrete. Clean Sweep wax base sweeping compound keeps high-quality floors looking their best!

Ideal for use on:

  • Schools
  • Churches
  • Museums
  • Gyms

USDA certified petroleum free (regular and no-grit)

Green Sweep is an environmentally friendly, 100% USDA certified alternative to the traditional petroleum based compound. This compound is preferred green product for industries and consumers that want to leave a small pollution imprint by implementing green products in day to day business and work. Green Sweep is made as sanded grit or no-grit, and performs as well as the traditional petroleum oil base sweeping compounds.

This product is...

  • Economical
  • Environmentally friendly
  • Made from recycled materials
  • Contains no petroleum ingredients
  • Safe for all floor surfaces
